About

I'm a PhD Candidate in Political Science and Social Data Analytics at the Pennsylvania State University. I study international conflicts escalation, foreign policy, human rights, and I am interested in machine learning methods and text analysis with application to political and social information. My dissertation looks at the effect of hawkishness of political leaders on conflict escalation.

Check out my CV for more information on my work. You can also find my code on github.
